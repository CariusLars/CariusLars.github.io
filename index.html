RGB-D Video Generation with GANs.

Can dense depth maps be predicted just as well as RGB video frames? We extend progressively growing GANs to predict future RGB-D video sequences based on a series of input frames.
Microcontroller Hardware

Giant LED Sign.

A 2 meter long sign with almost 1000 LEDs, fully controlled with a microcontroller reachable using WiFi. It can display scrolling and static text in all colors, turn a living room into a club or display an animated beer-pong scoreboard.
Machine Learning

Latent Space Video Generation.

We experiment with generating continuous videos from single input frames by leveraging smooth latent spaces. We project the input frames into latent space, fit curves to the latent vectors, generate new frames by sampling and apply ML-based postprocessing.
Machine Learning

Cooperative Reinforcement Learning for Games.

Can cooperative behaviour be learned from scratch using Reinforcement Learning? In my Bachelor's Thesis, I train models in self-made multi-agent games to show that it is possible.
Augmented Reality

Augmented Reality for Food & Beer Orders in Stadiums.

Order food and beer while watching your favourite sport and get it delivered to your exact location. Our hackathon app utilizes AR to guide servers and communicate the customer needs. It's time to rethink indoor navigation!
